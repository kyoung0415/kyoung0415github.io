Home page

all pictures originated from my digital photography projects. Digital Photography was a semester-long interactive course, in which students expanded their knowledge about camera settings and adobe photoshop. All pictures were edited with Adobe Photoshop CS4 and I used manual mode on my Canon camera to capture the pictures. The inter-changeable settings in manual mode were used when necessary. Shutter priority was used to capture fast motion, and to achieve a suspended effect. ISO stands for International Standards Organization, which is a standardized industry scale for measuring sensitivity to light. Aperture controls the size of the diaphragm opening in a camera lens and regulates the amount of light that passes through the camera.
