Point of View Project

Eye Level Bird's Eye Worm's Eye

All pictures were translated to a grayscale, which entails that the pictures will lose their color and change to black and white. For the eye level picture and bird's eye picture, I played with contrast to result in a darker background, but a lighter subject matter. The worm's eye picture, resulted in the subject being darker and the background being lighter and slightly ombre, which was unintentional but I think adds to the picture's appearance.
